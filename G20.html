ABOUT THE COMMITEE

G20 is a group of 20 countries that came together after the failure of G7, an initiative taken by rich countries to form policies regarding world issues. Once this initiative failed, G20 was started- this time taking into consideration other developing nations as well.

This agenda aims to discuss the scarcity of food and how to improve agricultural practises to become more sustainable.
